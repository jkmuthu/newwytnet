# WytApps Overview

**WytApps** are the applications that power the WytNet ecosystem. Each app is a specialized tool designed to solve specific problems for individuals and organizations.

## What are WytApps?

WytApps are **Engine-created applications** built on the WytNet platform. They provide focused functionality across various domains including communication, productivity, finance, e-commerce, and more.

### Key Characteristics

- **Category-Based**: Apps can belong to multiple categories for flexible organization
- **Panel-Specific**: Each app is available for MyPanel (individuals), OrgPanel (organizations), or both
- **Individual Pricing**: Each app has its own pricing model
- **Bundle Support**: Apps can be bundled into WytSuites with special pricing
- **Integrated**: All apps work seamlessly together within the WytNet ecosystem

## MyPanel vs OrgPanel

**MyPanel** (Personal Workspace)
- Designed for individual users
- Personal productivity and lifestyle apps
- Examples: WytLife, WytGod, WytWorld

**OrgPanel** (Organization Workspace)
- Designed for teams and businesses
- Collaborative and business-focused apps
- Examples: WytHive, WytBiz, WytPay

**Both Panels**
- Available for both individual and organizational use
- Examples: WytPass, WytPanel, WytWall, WytAI

Users can seamlessly switch between MyPanel and OrgPanel based on their organization memberships.

## App Categories

WytApps span 17 distinct categories:

1. **Core Platform** - Essential platform features (WytPass, WytPanel, WytPoints, WytApps)
2. **Communication** - Messaging and calling (WytCall, WytMeet, WytMail, WytCast)
3. **Finance** - Payments and financial services (WytPay, WytWallet, WytInvoice, WytCoin)
4. **Business** - Business management tools (WytBiz, WytQuote, Esign Creator)
5. **Social** - Social networking and community (WytWall, WytCircle, WytHash)
6. **Productivity** - Task and project management (WytDuty, WytHive, WytForm)
7. **Marketplace** - App stores and marketplaces (WytApps, WytStore, WytShop)
8. **E-Commerce** - Online stores and selling (WytShop, WytStore)
9. **Development** - Coding and app building (WytCode, WytBuilder)
10. **Creation** - Content and website creation (WytSite, WytBuilder, WytForm)
11. **Utilities** - Tools and converters (WytQRC, Currency Converter)
12. **Lifestyle & Legacy** - Life continuity, spirituality, and experiences (WytLife, WytGod, WytWorld)
13. **Digital Assets** - Crypto and investments (WytCoin, WytGold, WytWallet)
14. **Storage** - File management (WytCloud)
15. **Analytics** - Tracking and insights (WytScore, WytTrace)
16. **Education** - Learning and assessment (DiscAssesser)
17. **Real Estate** - Property management (WytLand)

## Pricing Models

WytApps support various pricing models:

- **Included in Platform**: Core features like WytPass, WytPanel, WytPoints
- **Individual App Pricing**: Most apps have their own subscription price
- **Transaction-Based**: Payment apps charge per transaction (WytPay, WytCoin, WytGold)
- **Commission-Based**: Marketplaces take a percentage (WytStore, WytWorld)
- **Freemium**: Free with optional premium features (Currency Converter, WytGod)
- **Usage-Based**: AI apps charge based on usage (WytAI)
- **Tiered Storage**: Storage apps have capacity-based pricing (WytCloud)

### Bundle Pricing

Apps can be bundled into **WytSuites** with special bundle pricing:

- **WytWorks**: Productivity bundle (WytCircle, WytDuty, WytTask, WytGoals)
- **WytStax**: Accounting bundle (WytInvoice, WytPay, and more)
- **WytCRM**: Sales & marketing bundle (WytCircle, WytLead, WytQuote)

See [WytSuites Documentation](/en/wytsuites/) for more details on bundles.

## Pricing Plans Integration

The **Pricing Plans** system supports:

- Individual app subscriptions
- Bundle (WytSuite) subscriptions
- Mixed plans (individual apps + bundles)
- Custom enterprise pricing
