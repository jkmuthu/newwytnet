# WytWorks - Productivity Suite

**WytWorks** is a comprehensive productivity and collaboration suite designed for teams and organizations to manage work efficiently.

## Overview

WytWorks bundles essential productivity tools into a single, integrated workspace for:
- Task and duty management
- Team collaboration
- Goal tracking
- Community building

## Included Apps

### Core Apps

1. **[WytCircle](/en/wytapps/apps-catalog#6-wytcircle)** - Groups & Communities
   - Create and manage team circles
   - Focused project discussions
   - Member management and permissions
   - File and resource sharing

2. **[WytDuty](/en/wytapps/apps-catalog#16-wytduty)** - Task & Duty Management
   - Personal and team task lists
   - Duty assignment and tracking
   - Deadline reminders and notifications
   - Recurring tasks and schedules

3. **WytTask** *(Planned - Q1 2026)*
   - Advanced task management with dependencies
   - Gantt charts and timeline views
   - Sprint planning and agile workflows
   - Time tracking and estimates

4. **WytGoals** *(Planned - Q2 2026)*
   - Goal setting and OKR management
   - Progress tracking and metrics
   - Team and individual goals
   - Achievement milestones

### Integration Features

WytWorks apps are deeply integrated:
- **Shared Task Context**: Tasks created in WytDuty appear in Circle discussions
- **Goal Alignment**: Link tasks to broader goals (WytGoals)
- **Unified Notifications**: Single notification stream for all productivity events
- **Cross-App Search**: Search tasks, discussions, and goals from one place
