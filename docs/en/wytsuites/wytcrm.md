# WytCRM - Sales & Marketing Suite

**WytCRM** is a comprehensive customer relationship management suite designed for sales teams, marketers, and business development professionals.

## Overview

WytCRM bundles essential sales and marketing tools into an integrated CRM platform for:
- Lead management and tracking
- Customer relationship building
- Quotation and proposal generation
- Sales pipeline management

## Included Apps

### Core Apps

1. **[WytCircle](/en/wytapps/apps-catalog#6-wytcircle)** - Groups & Communities
   - Customer segmentation
   - Community building
   - Private client groups
   - Engagement tracking
   - Event organization

2. **WytLead** *(Planned - Q1 2026)*
   - Lead capture from multiple sources
   - Lead scoring and qualification
   - Automated lead nurturing
   - Lead assignment workflows
   - Conversion tracking

3. **[WytQuote](/en/wytapps/apps-catalog#21-wytquote)** - Quotation Management System
   - Professional quote builder
   - Product catalog integration
   - Approval workflows
   - Quote-to-invoice conversion
   - Client interaction tracking
   - Revision management

4. **WytPipeline** *(Planned - Q2 2026)*
   - Visual sales pipeline
   - Deal tracking and forecasting
   - Custom sales stages
   - Win/loss analysis
   - Revenue forecasting

### Integration Features

WytCRM apps work together:
- **Lead to Customer Journey**: Track prospects from first contact to loyal customer
- **Quote to Deal**: Link quotations to pipeline opportunities
- **Customer Communities**: Engage customers through circles
- **Unified Communication**: All customer interactions in one place
- **Automated Workflows**: Trigger actions based on customer behavior

## Sales Workflows

### Lead to Customer Journey

1. **Lead Capture**: Form submission → WytLead
2. **Qualification**: Lead scoring and assignment
3. **Engagement**: Email/call campaigns via WytMail/WytCall
4. **Quotation**: Generate quote in WytQuote
5. **Negotiation**: Track revisions and approvals
6. **Conversion**: Move to WytPipeline as opportunity
7. **Closing**: Convert quote to invoice (WytInvoice)
8. **Retention**: Add to customer circle (WytCircle)

### Automated Workflows
- Auto-assign leads based on territory/industry
- Send follow-up emails after quote
- Notify manager when deal reaches certain value
- Create tasks for sales reps automatically
- Escalate stale leads
