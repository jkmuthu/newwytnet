# WytStax - Accounting Suite

**WytStax** is a comprehensive financial management and accounting suite designed for businesses, freelancers, and accountants.

## Overview

WytStax bundles essential financial tools into a unified accounting platform for:
- Invoice generation and management
- Payment processing and tracking
- Financial reporting and analytics
- GST compliance (India)

## Included Apps

### Core Apps

1. **[WytInvoice](/en/wytapps/apps-catalog#20-wytinvoice)** - Invoice Generation & Management
   - Professional invoice templates
   - Automated invoice generation
   - Payment tracking and reminders
   - Client management
   - Multi-currency support

2. **[WytPay](/en/wytapps/apps-catalog#17-wytpay)** - Payment Gateway Integration
   - Multi-payment method support (UPI, cards, wallets)
   - Payment link generation
   - Recurring payments and subscriptions
   - Settlement and reconciliation
   - Transaction management

3. **WytBooks** *(Planned - Q1 2026)*
   - Double-entry accounting
   - Chart of accounts
   - Journal entries
   - Financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet)
   - Multi-company support

4. **WytGST** *(Planned - Q2 2026)*
   - GST invoice generation
   - GST return filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B)
   - Input tax credit management
   - E-way bill generation
   - HSN/SAC code management

### Integration Features

WytStax apps work together seamlessly:
- **Payment to Invoice Linking**: Automatic payment reconciliation
- **Real-time Books Update**: Invoice creation updates accounting books
- **GST Compliance**: Automatic GST calculations and reporting
- **Financial Dashboards**: Unified view of business finances
- **Bank Reconciliation**: Match transactions with bank statements
