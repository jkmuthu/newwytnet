## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

WytNet operates on a multi-tiered revenue model:

1. **SaaS Subscriptions**
   - WytNet Pro: Individual users
   - WytNet Teams: Small businesses
   - WytNet Enterprise: Large organizations

2. **Marketplace Commission**
   - App purchases
   - Module subscriptions
   - Transaction fees on WytWall

3. **White-Label Licensing**
   - Custom hub deployment
   - Branded platform instances
   - Custom domain support

4. **Professional Services**
   - Custom development
   - Integration services
   - Training and consulting

### Pricing Strategy

**Target Customer Segments:**
- Individuals: ₹499-999/month
- SMBs: ₹4,999-19,999/month
- Enterprises: Custom pricing

**Value Proposition:**
- All-in-one platform eliminating multiple subscriptions
- Cost savings compared to purchasing apps individually
- Integrated ecosystem with seamless authentication

## Competitive Analysis

### Market Position

**Direct Competitors:**
- Zoho One
- Microsoft 365
- Google Workspace

**Competitive Advantages:**
1. **Unified Identity**: WytPass provides seamless single sign-on
2. **Modular Architecture**: Pick and choose components
3. **White-Label Capability**: Full platform customization
4. **Indian Market Focus**: Tamil language support, local payment integration

### Market Opportunity

- **TAM (Total Addressable Market)**: 500M small businesses globally
- **SAM (Serviceable Available Market)**: 50M English/Tamil speaking businesses
- **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)**: 500K businesses in first 3 years
