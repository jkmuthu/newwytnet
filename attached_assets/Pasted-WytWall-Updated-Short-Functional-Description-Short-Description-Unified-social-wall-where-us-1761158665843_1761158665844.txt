WytWall – Updated Short & Functional Description

Short Description:

> Unified social wall where users post needs, offers, and life updates — connecting opportunities automatically.




---

Extended Functional Definition (for DevDoc / Replit manifest):

> WytWall is the dynamic social marketplace and interaction layer of WytNet.
It functions as both a public discovery stream and a personal intelligent feed, merging user needs, offers, updates, and activities across apps and networks.



Key Functional Highlights:

Public WytWall:

Displays all user-published Needs and Offers that are made public.

Automatically matches similar listings (e.g., a user’s need appears for others offering related products or services).

Includes Bucket List items — things users wish to do, buy, or achieve — visible to others who can offer related solutions.


Personal WytWall (Authenticated):

Shows matched opportunities specifically relevant to the logged-in user (based on their needs, offers, and interests).

Integrates updates from apps the user has installed; each app has a toggle — “Show in My WytWall.”

Streams connected social network updates (Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.) when linked via OAuth.

Acts as a unified timeline of the user’s WytNet and external digital activity.


Smart Matching Engine:

Matches Needs ↔ Offers automatically based on categories, location, and keywords.

Enables Offerers to view active Needs and bid or promote (via points).

Enables Needers to receive best-fit Offers directly in their WytWall feed.



Result:

> WytWall becomes the “Digital Pulse” of WytNet — combining marketplace, productivity, and social intelligence into one adaptive stream.
